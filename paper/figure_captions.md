# Figure Captions

## Figure 1. Cumulative identity-abuse risk over scenario timeline

This figure plots cumulative risk scores for the enriched manual-abuse and AI-assisted abuse scenarios. The dashed horizontal line represents the timeline detection threshold. The figure visualizes the central response-window problem: both abuse chains become detectable before export, but the AI-assisted scenario crosses the timeline more quickly and leaves less time for response.

## Figure 2. Intervention window before simulated SaaS export

This figure compares the number of minutes between first detection and simulated SaaS export for the enriched manual-abuse and AI-assisted abuse scenarios. In the current scenario design, manual abuse leaves a 28-minute intervention window, while AI-assisted abuse leaves an 11-minute intervention window.
